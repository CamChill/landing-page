Ocarina of Time is the greatest game of all time
The highly anticipated classic from the Zelda franchise was released in November 1998. It was an unprecedented adventure, being the first 3D installment in the series. To this day, it remains the highest critically acclaimed game of all time, with a score of 99/100 on Metacritic.
